Coffee Trivia

When did coffee first appear in Malaysia

Due to the influence of British colonisation during the 1820's, Malaysians were often more exposed to tea as the main beverage rather than coffee. It was later in 1898 when coffee culture started to grow as Chinese immigrants moved to Malaysia. (Source: Corporate Coffee Solutions)
